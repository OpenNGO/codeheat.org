About "In the Winter of Code"

There are many NGOs looking for experts in web or app development at a reduced price. Moreover, there are many NGOs situated as close as within a city, or as far apart as around the globe, that require similar or the same kind of apps. But most of the time NGOs are not able to afford a developer to complete their goals.

We at OpenNGO intend to bring NGOs, Developers, students and sponsors onto one single collaborative platform and solve the problems of NGOs. We intend to achieve this by being an Open Source Organisation to which projects can be proposed by various NGOs. We obtain contributors who could work on these projects which could in fact be used by other NGOs as well. Hence, workforce divided.

In the Winter of Code is a coding contest for projects by some NGO's. Contributors/Students could collaborate with our projects and help various NGOs with their technical requirements. These projects undoubtedly would help NGOs work and grow better, hence enabling you to contribute towards betterment of our nation. You also stand a chance of winning stipends, prizes and many more exciting goodies.
